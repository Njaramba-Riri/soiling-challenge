Our Story.

WeLove was born out of a shared passion for making a meaningful difference in the lives of persons with disabilities (PWDs). Our journey began at KISE during a hackathon, where a domain experts, various stakeholders and a group of students came together with a common goal: to harness technology and innovation to address the challenges faced by PWDs.

Inspired by our compassion and empathy for individuals with disabilities, we embarked on a journey of exploration and discovery. Through collaborative brainstorming sessions and tireless effort, we honed in on the pressing issue of toilet training for disabled children particularly children with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). Recognizing the significant impact that effective toilet training can have on a child's independence and quality of life, we were determined to find a solution.

Driven by a commitment to making a positive impact, we embarked on a collaborative effort to harness the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning in creating our unique toilet training assistance app. Through insightful conversations and in-depth research, we gained a deep understanding of the complex needs and struggles faced by children with ASD and their caregivers during the toilet training process.

Drawing from our collective expertise and experiences, we designed WeLove to be more than just a toolâ€”it's a beacon of hope and empowerment for families navigating the challenges of toilet training. By providing personalized recommendations, progress tracking, and educational resources, we strive to offer comprehensive support tailored to each child's unique journey.

Our team is driven by a shared mission: to transform the lives of disabled children and their families by equipping them with the tools and resources they need to succeed. With dedication, innovation, and compassion at the core of everything we do, we are committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

As young professionals, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in our communities and beyond. WeLove represents our commitment to innovation, inclusivity, and compassion, and we are proud to be part of this transformative journey.

Our core values.

    At WeLove, we embody:-

  • Empathy
  • Inclusivity
  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • Empowerment
  • Integrity
Empathy

We believe in understanding and empathizing with the experiences and challenges faced by individuals with disabilities and their families. By putting ourselves in their shoes, we strive to develop solutions that are truly impactful and meaningful.

Inclusivity

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where everyone, regardless of ability, is valued and respected. Our solutions are designed to be accessible and beneficial to individuals with a wide range of disabilities.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and creativity as drivers of positive change. Through continuous research, development, and iteration, we seek to push the boundaries of what is possible and find new ways to improve the lives of individuals with disabilities.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork. By working together with individuals, families, caregivers, and experts in the field, we can leverage diverse perspectives and expertise to create more effective and impactful solutions.

Empowerment

We are dedicated to empowering individuals with disabilities to live fulfilling and independent lives. Our solutions are designed to provide them with the tools, resources, and support they need to overcome challenges, achieve their goals, and realize their full potential.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and transparency in everything we do. We are committed to earning the trust and confidence of our users, partners, and stakeholders through ethical and responsible conduct.
